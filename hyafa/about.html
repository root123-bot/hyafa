We are the Best Coaching & Training Center

HYAFA Coaching and Training Centre is a capacity building centre established for the purpose of facilitating practical training on Accounting, Bookkeeping, Auditing, Taxation, Finance, ICT and Management consultancy activities.

The centre involves the use of certified professional expertise from reputable consulting firms or companies in imparting market-driven practical knowledge to trainees.

The objective of the centre is to nurture and equip trainees with the contemporarily skills needed in the market in order to enhance career building and competency in their areas of expertise.
